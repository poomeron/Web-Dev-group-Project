Welcome to our UN Sustainability Goals Site

    This website was created as a group project to explore three of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals: Goal 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy), Goal 13 (Climate Action), and Goal 15 (Life on Land). Our aim is to explain what these goals are, why they matter, and how everyday actions can contribute to a fairer, greener and more sustainable future.

    What you can find on this site

    • Goal pages: Dedicated pages for Goals 7, 13 and 15 that explain each goal, key targets, and practical “things you can do”.
    • About page: A clear overview of how these three goals connect and why they're important globally and locally.
    • Newsletter: A newsletter sign-up form where you can subscribe to receive regular updates and ideas for climate-friendly actions.
    • Team page: An introduction to our project team and how we divided the work across the different pages.

    Our project goals

    • Inform: Present the three UN Goals in simple, accessible language so that anyone can understand the core ideas.
    • Inspire action: Highlight realistic, everyday steps that students and community members can take to support these goals.
    • Practice web development: Use HTML, CSS and JavaScript to build a consistent multi-page website that is responsive and easy to navigate.
    • Work as a team: Coordinate content, layout and styling so that all pages feel like part of one coherent site.

    How to explore the site

    Use the navigation bar at the top of the page. On smaller screens, the hamburger menu will open and close the navigation so you can quickly move between the Home page, the Goals, the About page, the Newsletter form and the Team page. All pages share the same layout and colour scheme so it feels like one unified experience.
